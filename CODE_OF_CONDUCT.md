# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Welcome diverse viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

Unacceptable behavior includes any conduct that is hostile, discriminatory, or makes others feel unwelcome or unsafe.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other community channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at <marcin@prodix.pl>. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.
